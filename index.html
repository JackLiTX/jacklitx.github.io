Course Overview

Across two modules you’ll learn the foundations of AI and how to apply them. We’ll go from data wrangling and evaluation to training a small CNN and deploying a model in the browser using TensorFlow.js.

What you’ll learn

  • Core ML concepts: features, labels, train/val/test
  • Neural network intuition & training
  • Evaluating models and avoiding common pitfalls
  • Responsible AI and data ethics
  • Deploying a tiny model to the web

Who it’s for

  • High school & early university learners
  • Developers curious about practical AI
  • Educators seeking project‑based content
